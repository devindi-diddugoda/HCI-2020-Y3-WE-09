Description

Enjoying direct beach access, Galadari Hotel is less than half a kilometer from local landmarks like Beira Lake and Khan Clock Tower. It is 27.4 km from Bandarainake Airport. Guests who drive enjoy free parking. The air conditioned guest rooms at Hotel Galadari have large windows that offer plenty of natural light. Rooms are equipped with a cable TV, tea/coffee making facilities and a personal safe. The Coffee Shop serves a daily buffet breakfast, while the rooftop California Grill offers fresh seafood and steaks. Other dining options include Chinese cuisine at Stix, Arabian food at Sheherezade Restaurant and pastries at Cafe 64. Drinks are available at the hotel's 2 bars and karaoke lounge. For a refreshing workout, guests can visit the well-equipped fitness center or enjoy a game of tennis. The Galadari also provides a tour desk and car rentals. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.7 for a two-person trip.

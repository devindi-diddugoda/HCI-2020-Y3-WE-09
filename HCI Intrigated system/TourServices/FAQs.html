Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to post my ad on your site?

All ads are absolutely free. Once you have used up your posting allowance for the month, you can either set up a Membership in order to post more ads, or wait until the first day of the next month when your allowance gets reset

Whom do I contact to inquire about an accommodation or car hire?

The contact details of the advertisers will be displayed on the More Details Page of each ad. Please click on the 'view Ad >>' link of each ad to visit this page.

I am visiting Sri Lanka on holiday. Will you be able to find me an accommodation or car hire?

Unfortunately we do not provide that service. Please browse/search our site for ads for your liking and contact the advertisers of those ads directly.

How do I post my ad on your site?

    All ads are absolutely free of charge on our site and you can post your ad by following the simple steps below.

  1.   You will need to register with our site. You will only need to enter your name, username, password and email address to register. You can register on this page :     http://www.lankaholidays.com/myaccount/regform.php?
  2.   Once registered you will have 3 buttons to select. Please click on the ‘Add a new Ad' button.
  3.   You will then need to select the category to which you are posting your ad (eg: Accommodation, Car/Van hire, Tour/services etc.). Please click on the appropriate button to proceed
  4.  You can enter the details of your ad at this stage and press the SUBMIT button at the bottom of the page to proceed.
  5.  You will now be presented with two options – whether to finish posting the ad (without pictures) or add pictures. Kindly select the appropriate option

I have forgotten my password

If you have forgotten your password, please go to Lost Password page and enter your Username and Email. Your password will be emailed to you. If you cannot remember your Username, please Contact us.

My ad is on the site but I do not have login details

Please register with us to create a new account and re-submit your ad. Then let us know via the Contact us form your old ad so we can delete it.

What are upgraded and promoted ads?

Upgraded ads allow you to promote your ad to be placed permanently on the front page of the relevant category for a period of 30 days. To upgrade an ad, please login and click on the 'View / Edit / Del ads ' option, then click the 'Upgrade ' button next to the ad you wish to upgrade.

How do I edit my ad after I have posted it ?

You will need to Login and then select 'View / Edit / Delete ads '

How do I find the geo data for my ad?

  1. You will be able to find the Latitude or Longitude of your location from Google Maps or by following the simple steps below:
  2. Visit the site http://www.gorissen.info/Pierre/maps/googleMapLocation.php?lat=6.910067&lon=79.859619
  3. Select your location using the zoom and navigation buttons (on top left of map) and your mouse and once you have found it, click on your location. The Latitudes and Longitudes will be shown on the map itself and also on the right side. Copy that information into the appropriate boxes when adding/editing your ad. All ads are completely free!

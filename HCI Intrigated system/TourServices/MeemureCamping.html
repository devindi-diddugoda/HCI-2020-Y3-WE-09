Description

The population of Hikkaduwa is about 400 with roughly 100 families. This is yet another society struggling to maintain their unique culture. Most of the younger generation is migrating to the cities in search of jobs and better lifestyles. As more of them become aware of the opportunities available in the city, they pursue the possibilities of a more comfortable life, at the least.

Much of the youth are found working at garment industries or joining the armed forces. As unfortunate as it may be, they do not turn back to the village and often express shame to admit they are from such a remote village.
